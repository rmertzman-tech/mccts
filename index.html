Purpose

From felt certainty to public reasoning

This studio teaches students how to move from immediate moral experience into disciplined shared reasoning without treating emotion as the enemy of thought.

Core teaching claim:

Students do not abandon felt moral experience. They learn to translate it into public, testable, qualified, and repair-oriented reasoning.
